About the Event

With great succes held the conference " Graduates ! And then?” organized by EESTEC LC Xanthi in the context of “ JobFair Athens 2015 “. The goal was the connection between undergraduate students and labor market through talks with useful tips on CV preparation and conducting interviews. At the same time the undergaduate students were informed about the employment in research and development (R & D) and in industry. The world responded to our call as we counted more than 250 visitors from all parts of the Polytechnic School of Xanthi. Finally we would like to thank our speakers :
