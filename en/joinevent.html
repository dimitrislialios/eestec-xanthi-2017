WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW

  • Every member of LC Xanthi has the opportunity to join an International Event. A member could be every undergraduate, postgraduate student of the Democritus University of Thrace.

  • If you are not a member of LC Xanthi and you wish to join an event you need to go to Join Us section where you can find info how to join our commitment. Signing up is completely free!

  • Your participation in most events is free, excluding some that inform you for a small participation fee, before you apply.

  • You can be informed about upcoming events with the following ways:

    - Our mailing list.
    - Posts on our open Facebook group, EESTEC Xanthi. (don’t hesitate to join us!)
    - Main eeStec site (you have to create an account).

    •  

    PROCEDURE

    • First you have to register on eestec.net (press here to see how)

    • Go to “Events” section.

    • Select the workshop you are interested for and press “Details”.

    • Press “Apply to Event”

    • In the pop-up window fill your motivation letter with basic info about yourself, and why you want to participate in the specific workshop etc.
