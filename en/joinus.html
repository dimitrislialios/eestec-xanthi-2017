JOIN US

• Go to eestec.net and press “Register”.

• Give your personal info (with Latin characters) and press “Sign up!”.

• Go to your email (the one you provided on the sign up form) to validate your registration and Login to the website with the info you provided.

• Go to eestec.net/cities/xanthi and press the blue button “Join”, to join LC Xanthi.

 

Also, fill in the following form so you get emails from our LC with upcoming events and news.

  • With your registration at eeStec, you have the chance to participate in Local and International events in all over Europe, to take part in organizing workshops, to participate in Design and Technology Teams, get involved in soft skills trainings, take part in competitions and, of course, to improve your Curriculum Vitae (CV)!

  • Every member of LC Xanthi has the opportunity to join an International Event. A member could be every undergraduate, postgraduate student of the Democritus University of Thrace!

  • Registration is completely free!
