About the Event

The App Development Workshop took place in the University grounds of the Democritus University of Thrace at 16-17 May and was a huge success. The workshop, which was carried out by the Research Centre Athena, the Special Engineering Laboratory of DUTh and by EESTEC Xanthi, was about introducing the iOS environment, giving basic information before the development of an application and lastly, the creation of a model app from scratch. The attendance was impressive throughout the whole workshop. Pre-graduate and post-graduate students from a variety of universities throughout Greece, PhD candidates and professionals attended the lectures , acquiring useful knowledge and gaining experience on subjects such as the marketing, monetizing, performance metrics and of course development of an app.

The main lecturer was Lazaros Vrisis (Electrical Engineer and PhD candidate of AUTh), co-founder of Play compass Entertainment (a company with many apps on Apple Store). EESTEC Xanthi would like to thank everyone who attended the workshop, the Research Centre Athena and the Special Engineering Laboratory for the co-operation, and last but not least, every volunteer who contributed to the execution of the workshop.
