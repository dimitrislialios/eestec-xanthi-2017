About the Exchange

Do you love the sea? Do you like the summer sun? Have you always wanted to visit our famous Greek islands? Well...now you have the opportunity you have been waiting for!
After our succesful workshop “Plan B : Hack The System”, EESTEC LC Xanthi strikes again with a summer exchange. 
“Aquaholics” assure you that you’ll spend five stunning days from 20th to 24th of June, -just after HoBoM- in the beautiful island of Thassos, where you can also meet some International Board members.

Thassos is located in northern Aegean and is considered to be one of the most popular summer destinations. Its extraordinary beaches with crystal, clear waters and the captivating natural beauty make it a real paradise which waits to be discovered.

Of course, we have prepared many joyfull activities to make this event one to remember!
During your stay in Thasos you will have the chance to try hiking, water sports and explore the hidden beaches with cruises around the island. And that’s not all. Νights will also be memorable with crazy parties that will bounce you off the wall. Last but not least , you will have the chance to try the delicious greek dishes like gyros, souvlaki, tzatziki, feta as well as drinks like ouzo, tsipouro and raki.

We will depart from Xanthi on 20 June at 10:00 am so you should be here before 09:00 am. We can also accommodate you the night before.The participation fee includes accommodation in a hotel ,all meals and Xanthi - Thassos round trip. The number of participants will be limited due to advance hotel booking. So hurry up, pack your swimsuits and bring your EESTEC spirit!

Get ready to become an “Aquaholic” and LIVE YOUR MYTH IN GREECE !
