About the Event

One day before the official start of the HOBoM (HandOverBoardMeeting), the local board had the joy of attending a training based on the management of an LC. The training was carried out by one of the most experienced trainers and former member of the EESTEC International Board, Marta Polec from EESTEC Krakow, and by the first ever EESTEC LC Xanthi trainer, Maria Kouneli. The members of the local board would like to thank both of them for the very interesting presentation and for the useful advice that they gave them for the future.
